  • Fiduciary Governance by Paul Miller and Andrew Gold.
  • Information Fiduciaries and the First Amendment by Jack M. Balkin.
  • Trust Law as Fiduciary Governance and Asset Partitioning by Robert H. Sitkoff
  • Dynamic Fiduciary Dutiesby Andrew S. Gold
  • The economics of ownership, access, and trade in digital data by Nestor Duch-Brown, Bertin Martins, and Frank Mueller-Langer
  • A Story of Three Bank-Regulatory Legal Systems: Contract, Financial Management Regulation and Fiduciary Duty by Tamar Frankel
  • Compensation for Breach of the General Data Protection Regulation by Eoin O'Dell
  • Roads and Bridges: The Unseen Labor Behind Our Digital Infrastructure by Nadia Eghbal
