We're building the public domain into the purpose domain.

Digital Public's mission is to preserve, protect, and advance the social purpose of digital projects.

Causeway
A digital stewardship platform.

Causeway is a platform for stewarding public interest data and technology projects. We help projects find new homes, and ensure that they continue to support the communities that they were intended for.

When you upload your project to Causeway, we don't keep it. We put it in a digital civic trust, a legal framework to own something on behalf of a community. We maintain a copy of your project in cold storage until you sunset the project. If something happens to you, your organization, or your project, we'll find the project a new caretaker (or multiple new caretakers) that keeps your movement going. And you can make promises to your users that last.

Sign up for early access, coming fall 2018.

The Digital Civic Trust
Use our trusts to make your projects trustworthy.

Causeway uses digital civic trusts, a legal framework developed by Digital Public. On Causeway, trusts help ensure that your project benefits the community that you built it for. In other words, when you put your project on Causeway, you're not giving it to us: you're giving it to your users.
